Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rent a restroom trailer if I don’t have a power or water supply nearby?

Yes, you can. Greenflush Portable Toilet Rental will provide a power generator and a clean water supply to ensure your restroom trailer runs smoothly throughout the entire event.

How do I dispose of the waste in your porta potty holding tanks?

You don’t have to worry about that! Greenflush Portable Toilet Rental takes care of it for you. We vacuum the waste into our portable septic tanks and restock your unit so it’s clean, fresh, and ready to be used again.

Do you offer porta potty cleaning services?

Yes, we do. Beyond rentals, we also help others clean their restroom trailers, porta potties, and other sanitation equipment. We restore the clean look of both the interior and exterior just like ours.

Can I rent a porta potty if my toilet breaks down?

Yes, you can. Our porta potties aren’t just for events, we also rent them out for personal use, including emergencies like home toilet breakdowns, especially during renovations.
